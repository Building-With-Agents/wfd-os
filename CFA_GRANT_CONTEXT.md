## Key Operational Notes
- Monthly reconciliation matches QuickBooks transactions against bank and credit card statements
- Provider invoices are validated against WSAC placement data and per-placement rates
- Anomalies are flagged at 90% budget threshold (warning) and 100% (critical)
- Credit card charges >$1,000 not found in QuickBooks are auto-flagged
- Terminated providers (WABS, NCESD, Riipen) should receive no further payments
