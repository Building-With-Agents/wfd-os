§ 200.333  Fixed amount subawards.

With prior written approval from the Federal awarding agency, a
pass-through entity may provide subawards based on fixed amounts up to
the Simplified Acquisition Threshold, provided that the subawards meet
the requirements for fixed amount awards in § 200.201.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
APPLICABILITY NOTES (not part of the regulation):

- The reference to §200.201 (fixed amount awards) is essential reading
  alongside §200.333. Among other constraints, §200.201 requires the
  fixed-amount subaward to provide "reasonable assurance" the relevant
  cost principles in Subpart E have been met (the deliverable's price
  reflects allowable, reasonable, and allocable cost), and prohibits
  cost-share or matching beyond a fixed dollar amount.
- For CFA's K8341 grant: the GJC training providers' "per placement"
  payment structure (e.g., $2,500 per verified placement to Ada,
  $3,222 per placement to Code Day) has the structural shape of a
  fixed-amount subaward. Verify that ESD (the K8341 pass-through to
  CFA) has provided written approval for any sub-subaward that CFA
  treats as fixed-amount, and that the per-placement contract sums
  do not exceed $250,000 to any single subrecipient.
- If the per-placement structure is, on examination, a procurement
  contract (under §200.331 substance test) rather than a subaward,
  §200.333 doesn't apply at all — but §200.318–.327 procurement
  standards do.
