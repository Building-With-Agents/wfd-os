============================================

CLUSTER COMPOSITION
-------------------
The Economic Development Cluster (header section 11.300 in the OMB
Compliance Supplement Part 4) typically includes:

Assistance), administered by the Department of Commerce's Economic

III. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
============================

The Compliance Supplement Part 4 organizes requirements as a matrix
across compliance-requirement categories. The 2024 Compliance Supplement
condensed the historical 12-item matrix into 6 compliance requirements:

  A. Activities Allowed or Unallowed
  B. Allowable Costs/Cost Principles
  C. Cash Management
  E. Eligibility
  F. Equipment and Real Property Management
  G. Matching, Level of Effort, Earmarking
  H. Period of Performance
  I. Procurement and Suspension and Debarment
  J. Program Income
  L. Reporting
  M. Subrecipient Monitoring
  N. Special Tests and Provisions

(The 2024 supplement consolidated some of these — e.g., Period of
Performance and Cash Management are now generally addressed under the
applicable cost-principles language. Verify the 2025 Supplement's
specific organization.)

Listing 11.307, the Supplement typically specifies:

subrecipient_monitoring, classification_200_331, cost_reasonableness)
